How it works

There are only three lanes, so every obstacle pattern leaves one deliberate escape route.

Every boss win unlocks the next place on a 100-stop world tour, and each stop gets a little harder.

Beat a boss to recruit one random animal driver. Rainbow drops during boss fights unlock new paint colors.

Bubble pickups give you 10 seconds of protection, so you can smash through obstacles without losing a life.
