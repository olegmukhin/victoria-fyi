# The Road Trip

Small browser game prototype built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Play

Open [index.html](./index.html) in a browser, or serve the folder with a local static server.

## Controls

- `Left` / `A`: move left
- `Right` / `D`: move right
- `G`: open the garage
- `Enter` / `Space`: start or restart

## Goal

Survive each road trip leg, reach the boss rig, then stay in its lane long enough to break its armor while dodging its attacks.

## Progression

- You start with one random animal driver out of 40 total.
- Beating a boss unlocks one random new driver.
- Rainbow drops during boss fights unlock one of 20 paint colors.
- Bubble pickups grant 10 seconds of obstacle protection.
- Every boss win unlocks the next stop on the 100-place world tour.
- Routes unlock in order from easiest to hardest, and you can only choose from the places you have earned.
- Use the garage menu to choose from the drivers, colors, and places you have collected.
